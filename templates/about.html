About Us

Making music chart history accessible to everyone

How often is this updated?
The chart data is automatically updated weekly to include the latest Billboard Hot 100™ charts. Updates typically occur within 24-48 hours of Billboard™ releasing new chart data.
Why was this made?
This tool was created to combat Billboard™ decision to remove all historical charts from public view and place them behind a paywall. Chart history is an important part of music culture and should remain accessible to fans, researchers, and the general public.
What data is included?
The database includes complete Billboard Hot 100™ chart data from 1958 to 2025, spanning the entire history of the Billboard Hot 100 chart. This includes song titles, artist names, chart positions, peak positions, weeks on chart, and comprehensive historical performance data.
How do I use this?
Visit the Billboard Hot 100™ page to view the latest charts, or use the artist search feature to analyze any artist's complete chart history. You'll receive a detailed analysis with downloadable data and interactive visualizations.
Is this free?
Yes, this tool is completely free to use. We believe chart data should be accessible to everyone.
Can I download the data?
Absolutely! When you analyze an artist's chart history, you'll have the option to download the data in CSV format for your own analysis and research.
Who built this?
This project is maintained by independent developers who are passionate about music history and open data. We believe that cultural data like chart history should be freely accessible to fans, researchers, and historians.
I love this, how can I support you?
Thank you for your interest in supporting this project! This platform is provided free of charge to ensure that chart data remains accessible to everyone. If you find this resource valuable and would like to help maintain and improve it, you can support the project through my Patreon.
